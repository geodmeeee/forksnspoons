### Game Details & Guide
Introduction to Pixel Gun Survival


Pixel Gun Survival is a popular browser-based game where players take on the role of an avatar who must scavenge for resources, craft guns, and fight against various enemies in a pixelated world.


Game Overview


In Pixel Gun Survival, players are dropped into a procedurally generated map with limited resources. The goal is to gather materials, craft weapons, and upgrade your gun to defeat the enemy waves. As players progress through the game, they can unlock new guns, abilities, and upgrades to improve their chances of survival.


How to Play


To play Pixel Gun Survival, follow these steps:
• Click on an avatar icon in the top left corner of the screen to enter the map.
• Use your mouse or keyboard to move your avatar around the map, exploring new areas and gathering resources.
• Collect materials such as wood, stone, and metal by clicking on trees, rocks, and other objects.
• Craft guns and upgrade them using resources collected throughout the game.
• Fight against waves of enemy pixels, which can be defeated with your upgraded gun.


Tips and Tricks for Success


Here are some tips to help you succeed in Pixel Gun Survival:
• Gather as much material as possible from the map to craft more advanced guns and upgrades.
• Experiment with different guns and strategies to find what works best for you.
• Keep an eye on your health and ammo levels, as running out can be fatal.
• Try to stay mobile by avoiding enemy waves and exploring new areas quickly.
